Why

Wing are a new breed of content agency - we combine the creative approach and production values of advertising, the narrative skills of film, the distribution power of TV with an in depth understanding of the online environment.


And WingMan is part of the Wing collective - we are Wing’s 'run and gun' busy little brother. With a faster turnaround, event coverage and B roll style offering, we create content that is less storyboard and agency and more coverage. However, as a part of Wing, expect nothing less than the highest level of pride and care taken in our work and a calm and organised crew and approach to any given production. As WingMan is a separate offering we work with smaller crews who are fast and efficient.

Having WingMan as part of our collective alongside Wing makes us a more comprehensive offering - Wing as a content agency and WingMan as more of a production company style.

We understand the ‘new’ - the digital space, its challenges and opportunities, what works and what doesn’t, how its audience behaves, and what’s hot and current.

We also understand the ’traditional’ - we have many years of experience in broadcast and TV production and we know how best to use it
